Anya Forger

The deuteragonist of the series. Formerly known as Test Subject "007", she is a telepath whose abilities were created by an unknown organization. She is currently a student at Eden Academy and the adopted daughter of Loid Forger and Yor Forger.

Anya is a short young girl, height of 99.5 cm, with fair skin and green eyes. A noticeable characteristic of anya is her light-pink hair that curls inwards and her horn-like ornaments. At Eden Academy, Anya wears the standard uniform with knee-high socks and black Mary Janes. She carries around her brown school bag with a sheep keychain attached to it. Meanwhile during physical education class (P.E), Anya wears the school gym uniform with her hair tied back with a red headband along with white socks and shoes.

She made her first appearance in chapter 1 of the manga series, and in episode 1 of the anime series. She is portrayed by the japanese voice actress Atsumi Tanezaki and english voice actress Megan Shipman.
