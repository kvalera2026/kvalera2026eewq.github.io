Yor Forger, aka Thorn Princess

The tritagonist of the series. She is the wife of Loid Forger and the mother of Anya Forger. While working as an ordinary clerk at Berlint City Hall, she also lives a secret life as the Garden's assassin with the code name "Thorn Princess". Garden is an assassin group that operates within Ostania.

Yor is an attractive tall young woman - age of 27 and height of 170cm - with black hair and fair skin. When at home she wears a backless red off-shoulder sweater. When she is outside, she wears a long beige coat with black buttons over her clothes. As an assassin, she wears a form-fitting halter-style black dress.

She made her first appearance in chapter 2 of the manga series, and in episode 2 of the anime series. She is portrayed by the japanese voice actress Saori Hayami and english voice actress Natalie Van Sistine.
