Loid Forger, aka Twilight

The main protagonist of the series. He works for his country Westalis as a spy under the code name "Twilight". For his current mission "Operation Strix", he marries Yor Briar and became the adoptive father of Anya Forger.

Loid is a handsome man with blond hair, blue eyes and a light skin. He is quite tall with a height of 187 cm, and weights 70-90 kg. His exact age is unknown but his apperance suggests that he is in his late 20s. When at home, he wears a normal shirt with long black pants. When he is outside, he wears a light green suit with a red tie. As a psychiatrist, he wears a lab coat over his shirt and tie.

He made his first appearance in chapter 1 of the manga series, and in episode 1 of the anime series. He is portrayed by the japanese voice actor Takuya Eguchi and english voice actor Alex Organ.
