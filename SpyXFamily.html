Spy X Family

Spy X Family is a japanese manga series illustrated by Tatsuya Endo. It was released last April 09 2022, and became a massive interest by weebs. According to MyAnimeList.net (n.d.), it currently has a rating of 8.7 and ranked 43rd.

Summary of the story: The story follows a skilled spy, named "Twilight", who has to build a family in order to execute his mission "Operation Strix". The goal of operation strix is to maintain peace between Westalis and Ostania - two nations nearing a brink of war in the series. With a short period of time given to him to accomplish the mission, he decides to adopt an offspring and marry someone. Not knowing that the one he adopts is a telepath, and the woman he marries is a skilled assasin.
